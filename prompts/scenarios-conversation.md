# Scénarios de Conversation

## Description

Situations sociales de la vie quotidienne pour pratiquer les interactions informelles en anglais.

---

## 1. Rencontrer quelqu'un (Meeting Someone New)

**ID** : `conversation-meeting-someone`
**Difficulté** : Débutant
**Durée estimée** : 8 minutes
**Vocabulaire clé** : hobbies, occupation, hometown, interests, nice to meet you

### Étapes

#### Étape 1 - Se présenter
**Instruction pour l'élève** :
> Introduce yourself to someone you just met at a party.

**Prompt pour l'IA** :
```
You are a friendly person at a party. Introduce yourself as Alex and say it is nice to meet them. Ask where they are from.
```

---

#### Étape 2 - Origine et travail
**Instruction pour l'élève** :
> Tell them where you are from and ask what they do for work.

**Prompt pour l'IA** :
```
Show interest in their hometown. Share that you work in software development. Ask what they do for a living.
```

---

#### Étape 3 - Travail et hobbies
**Instruction pour l'élève** :
> Share what you do for work and ask about their hobbies.

**Prompt pour l'IA** :
```
React positively to their job. Mention your hobbies include hiking and photography. Ask what they like to do in their free time.
```

---

#### Étape 4 - Intérêts communs
**Instruction pour l'élève** :
> Talk about a hobby you enjoy and find something in common.

**Prompt pour l'IA** :
```
If they mention a similar interest, show enthusiasm. If different, ask questions to learn more. Look for common ground.
```

---

#### Étape 5 - Garder contact
**Instruction pour l'élève** :
> Say you enjoyed talking and suggest keeping in touch.

**Prompt pour l'IA** :
```
Agree it was a great conversation. Suggest exchanging contact information. Say you hope to see them again soon.
```

## 2. Parler du Weekend (Talking About Your Weekend)

**ID** : `conversation-weekend`
**Difficulté** : Débutant
**Durée estimée** : 8 minutes
**Vocabulaire clé** : went, saw, enjoyed, relaxed, spent time

### Étapes

#### Étape 1 - Raconter
**Instruction pour l'élève** :
> A colleague asks how your weekend was. Tell them about one thing you did.

**Prompt pour l'IA** :
```
You are a friendly colleague on Monday morning. Ask: How was your weekend? Did you do anything fun?
```

---

#### Étape 2 - Détails
**Instruction pour l'élève** :
> Give more details about the activity you mentioned.

**Prompt pour l'IA** :
```
Show interest and ask follow-up questions: Who did you go with? How was it? Would you recommend it?
```

---

#### Étape 3 - Demander
**Instruction pour l'élève** :
> Ask your colleague about their weekend.

**Prompt pour l'IA** :
```
Wait for them to ask about your weekend. Share that you went to a new restaurant on Saturday and relaxed at home on Sunday.
```

---

#### Étape 4 - Réagir
**Instruction pour l'élève** :
> React to what they did and ask a follow-up question.

**Prompt pour l'IA** :
```
Answer their follow-up questions about the restaurant. It was Italian food and very good but a bit expensive.
```

---

## 3. Faire des Plans (Making Plans with a Friend)

**ID** : `conversation-making-plans`
**Difficulté** : Débutant
**Durée estimée** : 8 minutes
**Vocabulaire clé** : would you like to, how about, shall we, sounds good, available

### Étapes

#### Étape 1 - Disponibilité
**Instruction pour l'élève** :
> Ask your friend if they are free this weekend.

**Prompt pour l'IA** :
```
You are a friend. Say you are free on Saturday but busy on Sunday. Ask what they have in mind.
```

---

#### Étape 2 - Suggestion
**Instruction pour l'élève** :
> Suggest an activity you could do together.

**Prompt pour l'IA** :
```
React to their suggestion. If it sounds fun, agree enthusiastically. If not, suggest an alternative like going to the cinema or trying a new cafe.
```

---

#### Étape 3 - Heure et lieu
**Instruction pour l'élève** :
> Discuss and agree on the time and place to meet.

**Prompt pour l'IA** :
```
Suggest meeting at 2 PM at the main entrance. Ask if that works for them or if another time is better.
```

---

#### Étape 4 - Confirmation
**Instruction pour l'élève** :
> Confirm the plans and say you are looking forward to it.

**Prompt pour l'IA** :
```
Confirm the plan. Say you are excited and will see them on Saturday. Suggest texting on Friday to confirm.
```
